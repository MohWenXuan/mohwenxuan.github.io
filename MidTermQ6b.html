Ingredients of Curry Chicken

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/1-2 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves


Steps of Cooking

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragnant
  2. Add in chicken
  3. Continue to saute until chicken release oil
  4. Add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt
  5. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly
  6. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate
  7. Remove and serve
