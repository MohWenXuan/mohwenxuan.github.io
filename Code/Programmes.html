ABOUT HOGWARTS COURSES

Courses We Offer

We understand there is a lot to consider when choosing the right course for you. Here is all the information you need to know to make sure you are aware of what courses and programs are offered at the University of Hogwarts so that you can apply with ease

Diploma in Law

In the Diploma in Law program, students learn about the nature of law, legal institutions, branches of the law, and the roles of the law in society. Diploma in Law combines the expertise of academicians and legal practitioners to provide the latest information on legal developments. It was developed by the Academic Board of Hogwarts University together with a panel of external examiners.

Diploma in Multimedia

In this program, students will gain exposure to a comprehensive multimedia education that integrates theoretical, creative, conceptual, and technical aspects of design and development with modern technology. In addition to teaching students about design and motion graphics, it also teaches them how to develop a wide range of skills in video and sound editing, prototyping, graphic production, and interactive design.

ACCA

ACCA is renowned globally for its rigorous, high-quality training, which is respected by multinationals and public-listed companies, professional firms, and government institutions. If you are able to complete the challenging course of ACCA, employers around the world will perceive you as a professional, technically proficient, and experienced individual.
